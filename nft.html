Why NFTs?

We honestly think that 99% of NFT projects that are currently out on the market do nothing to increase human agency. We feel it's time to change the course and educate users about BCI, despite being a neglected topic. Here at AE we are already developing software components of BCI. Specifically, we are developing machine learning algorithms to interpret brain activity and real-time decoding systems. We will be the leaders in the space to fight against big tech and inspire them to place human agency at the center of their objectives. Our one and only mission is to create human agency by increasing agency. NFTs will help create awareness and allow for more collaborations with research groups around the world to advance the state of the art and maximize the positive impact of BCI.
